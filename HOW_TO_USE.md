## For Participants (Joining a Secret Santa Group)

### Step 1: Get the Invite
Your organizer will send you either:
- A **link** (tap it to open the app directly)
- A **group code** (you'll enter it manually)

### Step 2: Join the Group
1. **Open DrawJoy**
2. **Tap "Join Existing Group"**
3. **Enter the group code** (if you didn't tap a link)
4. **Fill in your details:**
   - **Your Name**: How you want to appear
   - **Email (Optional)**: To get notified when names are drawn
   - **Wishlist (Optional)**: Gift ideas for your Secret Santa!

**Wishlist Tips:**
- Be specific: "Mystery novels" not just "books"
- Include price ranges: "Coffee mugs ($10-15)"
- List alternatives: "Board games, puzzles, or cooking gadgets"
- Include sizes if relevant: "Shirt size: M"

### Step 3: Wait for the Draw
- You'll see other participants joining
- Wait for the organizer to draw names
- You'll get an email notification (if you provided one)

### Step 4: See Who You're Buying For
1. **Open the app** after names are drawn
2. **Go to your group**
3. **See your assignment** in the highlighted card
4. **Read their wishlist** for ideas
5. **Shop and wrap!** ğŸ

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't see who I drew"
- Make sure names have been drawn (organizer needs to tap "Draw Names")
- Refresh the group screen
- Close and reopen the app

### "Draw Names button is disabled"
- Need at least 2 participants
- Make sure everyone has finished joining
- Check if names already drawn (only once per group)

### "Invalid group code"
- Double-check the number
- Ask organizer for a new link
- Make sure you're typing just numbers

### "I want to change my wishlist"
- Currently can't edit after joining
- Ask organizer to create new group
- Or just text your Secret Santa directly (defeats purpose though!)

### "Can I see everyone's assignments?"
- **No!** That's the point of Secret Santa
- Only the server knows the full list
- Even organizers can't see all assignments
